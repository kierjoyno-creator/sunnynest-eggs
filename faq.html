Which chicken egg carton should I buy first?

Most first-time buyers start with the Farmhouse Brown Dozen because it is familiar, flexible, and easy to reorder. Premium home cooks often choose the pasture-raised 18 pack, while bigger households move up to the 30-egg tray.

How fresh are the chicken eggs when they are packed?

SunnyNest Eggs is built around daily collection, careful shell checks, and cold packing before route dispatch so the eggs arrive feeling ready for immediate kitchen use.

What happens after I reserve my egg basket?

Your order details are saved, the carton request is matched to the next route window, and the eggs are prepared for a chilled delivery flow.

Can I buy eggs for a bakery, cafe, or larger kitchen?

Yes. The bulk options on the shop page are there for higher-volume buyers who need a simpler way to order more eggs in one go.

How do I know when more eggs are available?

Join the egg alerts form and the website will save your email for route and restock reminders so you can come back before you run low.

Do I need to keep checking the website every day?

Not if you join egg alerts. That gives repeat buyers a softer, easier reason to come back at the right time instead of constantly checking availability.
